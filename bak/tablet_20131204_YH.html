Publication Policy
All national full page and greater ads that have not opted out by Order Close Date will be included in the tablet editions as a Straight from Print (SFP) ad unless the advertiser's intention to run Designed for Tablet (DFT) ad or Enhanced for Tablet (EFT) ad is communicated to AMEX Publishing prior to Ad Close date.

You may not opt out of one device and run on another. If you opt out, you are opting out of all devices. (iPad®, Kindle Fire®, NOOKColor® and Next Issue Media).

If there is a main URL in print, that URL will be activated on all devices this is inclusive of ads running straight from print (SFP) or designed for tablet (DFT) ad.

Flash is not supported on the iPad or Kindle Fire. HTML5 is now universally supported on the iPad and Kindle Fire.

All ads run in vertical orientation only.

SFP (Straight from Print):
Non interactive ads resized for tablet directly from the supplied print ad (PDF X1a). They are automatically centered and proportionately scaled to best fit the device in portrait orientation. SFPs may have only one URL link on the creative for web activation. If there are multiple URLs, the main corporate URL will be activated.

DFT (Designed for Tablets):
Non interactive ads designed to fit the 3:4 aspect ratio (iPad) and 6:19 aspect ratio (Kindle Fire) portrait orientation; DFTs may have only one URL link on the creative for web activation. If there are multiple URLs, the main corporate URL will be activated.

American Express Publishing will assist clients/agencies with Designed for Tablet (DFT) ads. Please check with your Sales Rep for production fees.

EFT (Enhanced for Tablets):
Interactive ads that are designed to fit the 3:4 aspect ratio (iPad) and 6:19 aspect ratio (Kindle Fire) devices. Ads can take advantage of multi media app functionality to play content such as image slide shows, video, audio and/or web elements. Multiple URLs may be activated.

PDF Samples of digital ads that have run in our publication are available upon request.
